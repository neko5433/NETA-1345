Easy Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

instructions

To make these delicious chocolate chip cookies, start by preheating the oven to 350°F. Melt ½ cup of butter in the microwave for about 40 seconds, making sure it’s melted but not hot. In a large bowl, combine the butter with ½ cup of granulated sugar and ¼ cup of packed brown sugar until smooth, then stir in 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract and 1 large egg. Add 1 ¾ cups of properly measured all-purpose flour, ½ teaspoon of baking soda, and ½ teaspoon of kosher salt, mixing until just combined into a soft, slightly sticky dough. Gently fold in 1 cup of semisweet chocolate chips, then scoop out about 1.5 tablespoons of dough for each cookie, placing them 2 inches apart on a baking sheet. Bake for 7–10 minutes, until the cookies are set and slightly puffy, with the centers looking a little underbaked for a soft, chewy texture.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F.
  2. Microwave the butter for about 40 seconds. Butter should be completely melted but shouldn’t be hot
  3. In a large bowl, mix butter with the sugars until well-combined
  4. Stir in vanilla and egg until incorporated
  5. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt. Please read the recipe note about properly measuring flour
  6. Mix dough until just combined. Dough should be soft and a little sticky but not overly sticky
  7. Stir in chocolate chips.
  8. Scoop out 1.5 tablespoons of dough (medium cookie scoop) and place 2 inches apart on baking sheet
  9. Bake for 7-10 minutes, or until cookies are set. They will be puffy and still look a little underbaked in the middle

Recipe

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar packed
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour properly measured
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
